In risk management, events are qualified by occurrence/likelihood and impact/significance. When you want to display a report, in many case a heat map is used to map the reported events against each other. The Excel sheet is an easy an quick demo on mapping events with a 2D mapping to likelihood and occurrence, which is frequently used in risk management excercises For this demo/example, some random generated data is used in Excel. Using the F9 function buttion, you can regenerate the random values and see the charts change.
